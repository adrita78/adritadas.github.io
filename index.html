Adrita Das

I'm a first year Biomedical Engineering Master's Research student at Carnegie Mellon University interested in studying machine learning applications in protein engineering. My research interests broadly lie at the intersection of protein design, drug discovery and AI. Previously, I studied Bachelor of Technology in Biotechnology from VIT Vellore, Tamil Nadu and worked under Dr. V. Sabareesh for my bcahelor's thesis.

In Professor Amir Barati Farimani's lab (MAIL LAB) at CMU, I am developing deep learning approaches for protein design. I've previously worked at the Molecular Biophysics Unit (MBU) at the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore under the supervision of Dr. Siddhartha Sarma on NMR Spectroscopy and MD simulation of proteins.
